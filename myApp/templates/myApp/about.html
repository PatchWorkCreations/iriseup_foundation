OUR STORY

We are ambassadors for belonging.

A non-profit community supporting programs focused on mentorship, education, and empowerment.

Why We Started

iRiseUp Foundation was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves to belong, to thrive, and to rise above their circumstances. Founded in 2021, we began with a vision to create spaces where individuals and communities could find hope, healing, and empowerment.

What started as a small community initiative has grown into a global movement, touching lives in the United States and the Philippines. We've learned that transformation happens when people feel seen, valued, and supported.

Where We Are Now

Today, iRiseUp Foundation supports multiple programs across education, leadership development, mental health, disaster relief, and youth empowerment. We've reached hundreds of children, empowered countless youth, and served communities on two continents.

But numbers only tell part of the story. The real impact is in the transformed lives, the restored hope, and the communities that have learned to rise together.
