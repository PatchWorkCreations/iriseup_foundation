What is iRiseUp Foundation?

iRiseUp Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to creating spaces where individuals and communities can find hope, healing, and empowerment. We support programs focused on mentorship, education, leadership development, mental health, disaster relief, and youth empowerment across the United States and the Philippines.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes! iRiseUp Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, so all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes.
